Common questions
Everything you need to know before you commit.
What is a credit and how do I know how many I need?

One credit equals one email sent. Verifying one email costs 5 credits. Warming one domain for one day costs 10 credits. AI features cost 2–10 additional credits per action.

A typical Growth user sends 200 emails/day with AI personalisation and verifies 500 addresses per month. That is roughly 6,000–8,000 credits per month — exactly what the Growth plan covers.

Do unused credits roll over to the next month?

On the Growth and Scale plans, unused credits roll to the next month as long as you renew on time (within 3 days of expiry). On the Starter plan, credits do not roll over.

We send renewal reminders 7 days, 48 hours, and 24 hours before expiry so you are never surprised.

Is Moxwarm and Moxcheck included in the subscription?

Yes — every Moxsend plan includes Moxcheck verifications and Moxwarm domain warmup. They share the same credit pool. There is no separate subscription for either.

If you only need Moxcheck or Moxwarm without Moxsend, standalone plans are available separately.

Does Moxsend work for Arabic and GCC markets?

Moxsend is built for the GCC market first. The interface supports full Arabic RTL layout with one click. The AI personalisation engine is trained on Gulf communication patterns — it knows that a short reply is not low intent, and that Friday silence is not disengagement.

Sending schedules automatically respect Friday prayer windows, GCC national holidays, and Ramadan working hours across UAE, Saudi Arabia, Bahrain, Qatar, and Kuwait.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

Cancel any time. Your plan runs until the end of the billing period you already paid for. After that, unused credits expire and your account moves to a read-only state — you can still access all your campaign data, contacts, and reports.

If you reactivate within 90 days, your campaign data is restored. After 90 days, it is archived.

Can I try before buying? Is there a free trial?

Every plan starts with a 300-credit free trial. No credit card required. 300 credits is enough to verify 60 email addresses, warm one domain for three days, and send 10 fully personalised cold emails.

That is the complete Moxsend stack — verify, warm, send — experienced in under an hour.
