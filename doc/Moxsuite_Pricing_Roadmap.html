A
Show the prompt at 91% usage — not 100% Timing
At 100% they are blocked and frustrated. At 91% they are still engaged and can act before it hurts. The credit meter at 91% filled is also psychologically unsettling — most users will upgrade rather than wait for the block.
B
Never show a pricing page — show an upgrade button Friction
Every click is friction that causes drop-off. The in-app upgrade prompt should collect payment and activate the new plan in the same flow — no redirection to the pricing page, no re-entering details, no confirmation screen delay. One click. Plan upgraded. Campaign resumes.

Never show lifetime plans publicly Strategy
Public lifetime deals attract deal hunters — people who buy once, never convert to subscription revenue, and clog your support queue. Inside the app means only genuine users who have already experienced the product see it. Their lifetime purchase price reflects real value, not desperation for cash.

Execution Roadmap
Build this in order.
Not all at once.
Each stage is a milestone, not a feature. Ship Stage 1 and 2 on day one. Stages 3 through 6 unlock as you hit the user milestones that make them necessary.
1
Month 1 — Launch
Public pricing page + free trial gate
Three tiers live. Free trial with 300 credits, no card required. Welcome email sequence (Day 1, 3, 6). This is all you need to start acquiring paying customers. Do not build anything else until you have 50 paying users.
✓ Target: 50 paying users in Month 1
2
Month 2 — Conversion
In-app upgrade prompts at 91% credit usage
Build the three upgrade prompt variants. Wire them to real credit consumption. Add annual billing option with 20% discount. This is the biggest single improvement to monthly revenue you can make — most SaaS products see 30–40% of upgrades come from in-app prompts, not the pricing page.
✓ Target: 40% of upgrades from in-app prompts
3
Month 3 — Retention
Standalone Moxcheck + Moxwarm pages
Launch the standalone product pages. Link from the "Just need one tool?" text below the pricing tiers. These pages serve double duty — they capture customers who would not buy Moxsend alone, and they are the most natural entry point into the full Moxsend funnel.
✓ Target: 20% of standalone buyers upgrade to Moxsend
4
Month 4 — Loyalty
Lifetime credits — visible only inside the app
Unlock the lifetime credits page inside the app sidebar. Send the Month 3 loyalty email to all existing subscribers. This email will convert 15–25% of your 3-month subscribers into a lifetime purchase — immediate cash and long-term commitment.
✓ Target: 15% of 3-month users buy lifetime
5
Month 5–6 — Enterprise
Enterprise contact page + sales motion
Build the enterprise page. Hire or designate one person to handle enterprise inquiries. The first enterprise deal will likely come from an existing Growth or Scale subscriber whose company has grown. The contact page is the last stage — but potentially the largest revenue per customer.
✓ Target: First enterprise deal by Month 6
6
Month 6+ — Expand
Add Moxcrm as a paid add-on to existing plans
Once Moxcrm is built, offer it as a $29/month add-on to any existing plan. Existing paying subscribers are your warmest audience. The upgrade email subject line: "Every reply from Moxsend now has a home." This is your first cross-product upsell and the beginning of the suite economics.
✓ Target: 25% of Scale users add Moxcrm
